Chapter 01

The estate,
in a single breath.

We began in 1992 — not with brochures, but with blueprints drawn by the hand of a father and the patience of a decade.

Thirty years and seventeen blocks later, Tariq Gardens has become one of Lahore's quiet institutions. Not the loudest housing scheme. Not the showiest. Simply the one where residents stay, and their children return to raise their own.

An LDA-approved master plan. A water network fed by twin tube-wells. A jamia masjid of state-of-the-art craft. A park in every block. And families who still say salaam when they pass.

Chapter 03

Everything considered.

01

Grand Jamia Masjid

A state-of-the-art masjid crowning the estate, serving residents and the surrounding societies alike.

02

Pure Water Supply

Independent distribution fed by twin tube-wells and an overhead reservoir — clean water, always.

03

Advanced Sewerage

A hygienic network connected to a dedicated disposal plant — complete drainage and sanitation.

04

Sui-Gas Network

Seamless domestic gas provision across residential zones, with capacity allocated for commercial use.

05

Wide Carpeted Roads

Generous 60 ft and 100 ft boulevards, carpeted and bordered by footpaths for an unhurried stride.

06

A Park in Every Block

Seventeen landscaped parks with jogging and walking tracks, designed around the lives of residents.

07

Independent Electricity

Powered via a dedicated feeder with luminous street lamps lining every road.

08

School · Hospital · Lawn

Demarcated zones for education, healthcare, and celebration — the village inside a city.
