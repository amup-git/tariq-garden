Our Story

Where Lahore's most discerning families call home.

Born from a vision to redefine urban living, Tariq Gardens Housing Scheme rose quietly on the outskirts of Lahore — a sanctuary where modern infrastructure meets timeless tranquility.

Developed on the guidelines of the LDA and shaped by the foresight of master planners, the community is woven into the landscape with purpose. Wide boulevards, generous parks, and thoughtfully zoned residences create a rhythm of life that is unhurried, considered, and deeply rooted.

More than a housing scheme — it is a state-of-the-art neighbourhood where neighbours become family and every evening feels earned.
